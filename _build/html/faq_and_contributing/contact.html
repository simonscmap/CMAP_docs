Contact and Contribute¶

Who to Contact¶

If you have questions about using Simons CMAP feel free to contact us at: cmap-data-submission@uw.edu

or preferably join our Slack channel

How to Contribute¶

You can contribute to pycmap or cmap4r github pages or make suggestions via email or Slack.
